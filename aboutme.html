About Me.

Hello there! I'm Panagiotis Xiropotamos, though my friends know me as Panos. Born and raised in Athens, Greece, I am now a 24-year old masters student at the National and Kapodistrian University of Athens. Graduated from the University of Patras as a Biologist, I now find myself at the intersection of Biology and Computer Science dedicated to translating raw data into meaningful knowledge, bridging the gap between computational analysis and biological relevance.

In addition to my professional and academic goals, I am deeply passionate about travel and food. Escaping the routine of Athens for brief two-day excursions provides me with relaxing breaks and the opportunities to explore the diverse landscapes of Greece, but never adverse to longer escapes on inspiring trips abroad. As for food, I find cooking to be a source of composure, offering a temporary escape from the stresses of daily life. I am always eager to try new cuisines, constantly seeking out culinary adventures that enrich my experiences.
